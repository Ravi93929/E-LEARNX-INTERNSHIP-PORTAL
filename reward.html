As part of your internship journey, you can earn exclusive rewards by completing various tasks and achieving milestones. Whether it's earning certificates, receiving swag items, or unlocking other exciting prizes, we’re here to celebrate your hard work and dedication!

How to Earn Rewards

To make your internship experience even more rewarding, you can earn different types of rewards based on your achievements during your internship. Here’s how it works:

1.Complete Tasks: Finish internship milestones like project submissions, skills assessments, or feedback surveys.

2.Achieve Milestones: Reach key performance goals or participate in events.

3.Redeem Rewards: Based on your performance, you will unlock rewards like certificates, swag, and more. The better you perform, the more rewards you earn!
